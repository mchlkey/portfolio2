Map

Made for Luiza Dale's Core Studio III Class, which involved a semester-long exploration of systems and their context within design. The system explored with this project is one of a brand with a controversial history, and how interpretations around the brand varied between perspectives. The guidebook explores the perspective of willing shoppers who are presented negative information about the brand, and are confronted with their choice to support the brand. 60 inch thermal print on reciept paper.
