Keyboard/Keyboard

https://mchlkey.github.io/Keyboard-Keyboard/

Made for Rasim Bayramov's Core IV Class. Keyboard/Keyboard serves as a website acting as a machine, where keystrokes are converted to piano keys. This translation turns sentences into song, breaking the bounds of a traditional songwriting practice. It's name comes from the connection between the 2 most common understandings of keyboards, which both are represented as instruments.
