Michael Wynne

Guide Book

Made for Luiza Dale's Core Studio III Class, which involved a semester-long exploration of systems and their context within design. The system explored with this project is one of a brand with a controversial history, and how interpretations around the brand varied between perspectives. The guidebook explores the perspective of a future employee and the accusations from previous employees about behavioral misconduct. 16 Page Risograph Saddlestitch Booklet.
