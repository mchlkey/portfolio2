Mark Making Pamphlet

Result of a half-semester research unit based around mark-making during Nicole Killian's Core Studio II class. This pamphlet touches on my process and how I found meaning within my marks. Corrosion and Signage are the 2 main themes that were explored within my practice. 8 Page Pamphlet Printed on 4 11" x 17" pages.
